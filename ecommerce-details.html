Developed a fully functioning eCommerce MERN stack web app providing the user a seamless shopping experience. Customers are able to easily sign-up for an account that allows them to add products to their shopping cart. The products are categorised so they may be easily located. On the checkout the user has the option of paying using the Stripe payment gateway.
